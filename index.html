My Everything

There isn't a better reason to be alive

Always beautiful. Always sweet.

In a garden of green, a sight so sweet,
A baby in yellow, a sunflower complete.
Petals of joy, in the form of a child,
Innocence and wonder, so pure and wild.

Wrapped in warmth, like the sun's gentle kiss,
In a world of discovery, nothing amiss.
Eyes wide with wonder, exploring the new,
A tiny sunflower, in a world so true.

Yellow petals dancing, in the soft breeze,
A baby's laughter, a heart at ease.
Nature's beauty, in this precious sight,
A sunflower blooming, in the morning light.

Recharging. A rare sight at night.

In peaceful slumber, she lies so still,
Arms reaching up, a sight to thrill.
Cheeks like roses, in the softest repose,
Dreaming sweet dreams, nobody knows.

Tiny fingers, curled in sleep,
Guardians of dreams, silent and deep.
Breathing softly, in a rhythmic flow,
A beautiful baby, in a world of her own.

With each gentle breath, a sigh of delight,
In the calm of night, everything's right.
A masterpiece of innocence, pure and true,
A sleeping beauty, in a world so new..
